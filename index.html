Facts about tulips:

  • There are 75 kinds of tulips.
  • Can grow up to like 70 centimeters.
  • Are known in Europe since the 16th century and came from the east.
  • It was one of the first speculative object in the netherland - see tulip mania.
  • There are several kinds of colors possible, like red, white, yellow and many more!
  • Tulips bloom in spring from march to may.
  • Can be symbolic used to show a fulfilling love!
